Wacky Waffles is a locally owned waffle restaurant with only one location in Los Altos, CA. It was founded in 1968 when Jane Fox came across an old building for sale. Her love for all things waffles inspired this popular restaurant.

Remodeled by her husband and father, Wacky Waffles finally opened in June 1969 and quickly gained popularity. It won Best Breakfast in the Bay in 1987, 1989, 1990, 1994, 2004, and 2012. Our menu has something for everyone, sweet waffles, savory waffles, and even waffles for dessert! Perfect for people of all ages and their families.

We plan on opening more locations around California so stay tuned for updates!
